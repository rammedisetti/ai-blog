Cancellation & Refund Policy

Learn about eligibility, process, and timelines for our refund and cancellation process.

Eligibility for Cancellation

  • Request within 7 days of purchase
  • Account in good standing
  • No prior refund issued for the subscription

Process for Requesting Cancellation

  1. Contact our support team with your order details.
  2. Complete the cancellation form we provide.
  3. Receive confirmation of your request.

Refund Terms & Processing

Approved cancellations will be refunded in full. Refunds are processed within 7 days of confirmation.

Refund within 7 days Refunded to original payment method

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Requests after 7 days of purchase
  • Accounts with violations of our terms
  • Bulk license purchases

Please review your subscription details carefully before purchasing.

Timeline for Refunds

Credit/Debit Card 5-7 business days
PayPal 3-5 business days
Bank Transfer 7-10 business days
